About Company

N-Joy was conceptualized in 2015 to be a facilitator of Fun based and Easy learning through the
use of creative and scientifically proven educational tool like Tangram. The idea germinated as a
compelling need to transform the traditional teaching methods to be more interactive, productive
and effective.
